Warranty Information

All products sold on our website are covered by the manufacturer's warranty, unless stated otherwise. The standard warranty period for electronic products, including TVs, is 24 months from the date of purchase.

If you experience any issues with your product during the warranty period, please contact us or the authorized service center listed in the product manual. Warranty coverage includes manufacturing defects but does not apply to damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs.

Return And Exchange Conditions

You have the right to request a full exchange of the purchased product within 14 calendar days from the delivery date, with the exception of personal hygiene items and kitchen accessories.

To be eligible for a return or exchange, the following conditions must be met:

  • The product has not been used.
  • It retains its original appearance,consumer properties, and factory seals and labels.
  • The sales receipt or invoice issued to the customer is presented.

Please note that returns or exchanges for non-food products of appropriate quality are accepted only if all the above conditions are fulfilled.

How To Return Or Exchange A Product

Products purchased in-store can be returned or exchanged at the same location where they were bought.
